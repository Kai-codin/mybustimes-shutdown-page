MyBusTimes V1 Has Been Shut Down

MyBusTimes V1 is officially retired. All your data has been transferred to MyBusTimes V2.

Note: Usernames with spaces in V1 now have underscores in V2. For example, Bill Bob is now Bill_Bob.

FAQ

Why was V1 shut down?

V1 has been replaced by V2 with better performance, features, and reliability.

Will I lose my data?

No. All companies, routes, and settings are safely moved to V2.

Do I need a new account?

No. Use your V1 login details for V2.
